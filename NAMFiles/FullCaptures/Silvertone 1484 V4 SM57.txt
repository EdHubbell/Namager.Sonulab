The Sears Silvertone Twin Twelve (Model 1484) is an iconic  "piggyback" tube amplifier manufactured by Danelectro for Sears, featuring 60 watts (closer to 35-40W RMS), two 12-inch speakers, and built-in tremolo and reverb. Known for its grimy, garage-rock distortion, it is favored by musicians like Jack White for its lo-fi, "brown" sound.
